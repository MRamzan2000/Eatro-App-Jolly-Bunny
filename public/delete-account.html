Delete Account & Data

You can delete your Eatro account and associated data in two ways:

Option A (Recommended): In the app

Open Eatro → Account Settings → Delete my account.

This immediately starts deletion via the in-app button.

Option B: Request via web form

Or email jollybunnyllc@gmail.com
We will verify ownership and complete deletion within 30 days.

Note: Advertising data (e.g., Google AdMob) is handled by Google. You can reset your Advertising ID in device settings.
